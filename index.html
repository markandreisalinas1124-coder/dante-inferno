The Nine Circles of Hell

1. Limbo 2. Lust 3. Gluttony 4. Greed 5. Wrath 6. Heresy 7. Violence 8. Fraud 9. Treachery

Circle 1: Limbo

The unbaptized and virtuous pagans. They are not tormented but live without hope of seeing God.

Circle 2: Lust

Sinners blown about by violent winds, symbolizing the power of lust to drive one uncontrollably.

Circle 3: Gluttony

The gluttonous lie in vile slush, representing the excess and waste of their indulgence.

Circle 4: Greed

The hoarders and the wasters, rolling heavy weights against each other for eternity.

Circle 5: Wrath

The wrathful fight each other on the surface of the river Styx, while the sullen gurgle beneath it.

Circle 6: Heresy

Heretics are trapped in flaming tombs for rejecting spiritual truth.

Circle 7: Violence

Divided into three rings: violence against others, self (suicides), and God/nature.

Circle 8: Fraud

The fraudulent are punished in ten ditches (Malebolge), each for a different kind of deceit.

Circle 9: Treachery

The traitors are frozen in ice, farthest from God's warmth and light.
